Welcome to Manali

Discover the scenic beauty of the Himalayas and immerse yourself in adventure and serenity.

Overview

Manali, nestled in the mountains of Himachal Pradesh, is a beautiful hill station famous for its snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and charming villages. It's the perfect escape for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and anyone looking to unwind in a serene environment.

Popular Activities

  • Trekking: Explore scenic trails like the Beas Kund Trek and Hampta Pass.
  • Paragliding: Soar over the beautiful Solang Valley and experience a thrilling aerial view.
  • River Rafting: Ride the rapids of the Beas River for an adrenaline-pumping adventure.
  • Hot Springs: Relax in the natural hot springs of Vashisht Village, believed to have healing properties.
